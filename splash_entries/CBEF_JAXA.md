# Cell Biology Experiment Facility (CBEF) — JAXA Kibo

**General**  
Operations: GROUND TELEMETRY + CREW INSTALLATION  
Category: Experiment Hosting Facilities with Centrifuges  
Operator/Vendor: JAXA / MHI (Mitsubishi Heavy Industries)  
Status: Active  
Flight Heritage: JEM Pressurized Module (Kibo), ISS  
Year of First Launch: 2008 (Space Shuttle, Kibo PM)  

**Specifications**  
Mass: Per JAXA/Kibo payload documentation  
Size: Rack-mounted; incubator unit with µG and 1G compartments  
Payload Volume Required: Standard medium canisters (6 in µG, 4 in 1G compartment)  
Power (Average): Per JEM rack; canister power: DC +5 V, +12 V, ±15 V  
Hardware Operational Temperature Range: 15°C to 40°C (controlled)  
Interface Definitions: Standard medium canister (power, analog, video, command bits per JEM spec)  
Radiation Sensitivity: Standard ISS  
Heat Sync Required: Facility thermal control  

**Research**  
Specimen Types Supported: Cells, plants, small animals (life science cultivation)  
Specimen Size/Quantity: 6 medium canisters (µG) + 4 medium canisters (1G); BEU-compatible  
Temperature Range at Sample: 15°C to 40°C (±1°C distribution, 0.1°C steps)  
Experiment Duration: User-defined; facility supports long-term cultivation  
Centrifuge: Yes (0.1 G to 2.0 G at 112.5 mm radius; 20–140 rpm, 1 rpm steps)  
Imaging: Video interface per standard JEM video specification  

**Logistics**  
Crew Handling Required: Yes (install/remove canisters)  
Autonomy: Yes (parameters set/changed from ground by telemetry; user-program automatic control)  
Sample Return Required: As per mission  
Compatibility with On-Orbit Sample Processing: Yes (Kibo PM, BEU standard canister interface)
